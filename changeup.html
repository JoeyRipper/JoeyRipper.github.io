Changeup overview

The changeup is an offspeed pitch, meaning it is used to screw up the hitter's timing at the plate. The changup also has some "drop" or "run" meaning that it moves differently than a normal throw. A changeup generaly moves 6-12 mph slower than the pitcher's fastball. With the different movement and similar release to a fastball this pitch could lead to the hitter being off balance during his swing, being good for the pitcher.
